mnml

Simple theme for deck.js with a tad of class.

  • Focus on your story, it's what matters most

  • Look the part, but don't spend hours tweaking the theme

Usage

Download the theme and import the CSS file:

<link rel="stylesheet" media="screen" href="path/to/mnml.css">

That's it! You're done.

Licensing
